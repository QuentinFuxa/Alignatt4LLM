\caption{\textbf{Operational view of the decoder-only cascade.}
A single decoder-only Gemma checkpoint is queried twice per input chunk: first
in the ASR role (audio$\to$text) to confirm a transcript prefix, then in the MT
role (text$\to$text) to extend the translation from the visible source span.
In the runtime this appears as two sequential vLLM requests with role-specific
AlignAtt observers ($\mathcal{H}_{\mathrm{ASR}}$ and $\mathcal{H}_{\mathrm{MT}}$),
each reconstructing only the draft-to-source block consumed by its gate. The
confirmed ASR transcript prefix feeds the MT source span, and the accepted target
prefix is reused as causal target prefill for the next MT step.}
\label{fig:dual-alignatt-cascade}
